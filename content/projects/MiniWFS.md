A persistent Unix-like, block-based filesystem implemented in C with inodes, allocation bitmaps, and direct plus indirect block addressing. Integrated with the VFS layer via FUSE for full file, directory, and metadata operations, plus POSIX-compliant timestamps and custom extended attributes.
