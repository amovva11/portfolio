A real-time voice fraud detection platform that streams live call audio and risk scores with low-latency voice and transcript processing. It pairs neural voice fingerprinting and AI speech detection with LLM-based transcript analysis — achieving **99% precision and 80% recall** — and flags AI-generated voices and social engineering attempts during live call simulations.
